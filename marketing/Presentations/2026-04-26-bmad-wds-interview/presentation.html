03   Scenarios

Journeys, not pages.

In WDS, the pages are the flowchart. No separate abstract diagram — the scenario is screen sketches arranged in flow, from start to desired state.

The Specification
Edge cases and business rules — in text, separately.
"Selective ignorance."
Focus on the best experience for the most valuable customers in the most valuable transactions. Design the sunshine path first. Edge cases come later — separately, so the core stays clean.
Selective ignorance is not laziness. It's strategic focus.
