BMAD

BMad is a game-changer for the solo builder.
It is not built for scale.

01

Agents live in repos, not in sync

Every team member has their own version. Different prompts, different behaviors, no shared state.

02

No framework for your own agents

BMad ships its standard agents. Building custom means starting from scratch every time.

03

Not built for scale

Clone repos, copy files, manual setup per machine. Not something you roll out to 40 people across London and EMEA.
