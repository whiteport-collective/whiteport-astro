## LinkedIn draft

You tried everything.

You encouraged. You equipped. You sat back, ready to enjoy the agentic explosion that was surely coming.

Nothing happened.

Give your designers a working AI prototype — they go back and redraw it in Figma.
Encourage your developers to build with AI — they spend more time fixing AI mistakes than they save.
Demo AI to management — they look back with glazed-over eyes.

Nobody is *against* AI. The opportunity is just perfectly shaped like a really sticky problem.

You've seen the solo builders shipping incredible products from their basements. One person doing the work of ten. Surely that scales to your team of thirty?

It doesn't.

I've spent the last weeks supporting three major Swedish brands across different industries. Same pattern every time. The blocker isn't tools. It isn't training. It's something else — and most leaders are looking past it.

What's blocking AI adoption in your team? Tell me in the comments.
