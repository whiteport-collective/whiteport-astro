## Prompt (long form)

A nice, stylish modern design studio — minimalist white walls, exposed concrete pillars, tall arched factory windows, monstera plants, mid-century walnut furniture, bookshelf with design books in the background. On a long live-edge wooden conference table sits a heavy, dusty, oil-stained orange-and-black gas chainsaw, freshly used. Sawdust scattered across the table. Wisps of grey smoke and exhaust curl up from the engine, slightly hazy in the air. The bar of the chain is dark with chain oil.

Standing around the table, four designers frozen in stunned silence:
- A man in a black wool turtleneck, round tortoiseshell glasses, neatly trimmed beard — half-eaten almond croissant in hand
- A woman in a tailored cream wool blazer, low ponytail, gold earrings — coffee cup paused mid-air
- A man in a beanie, oversized linen shirt, leather sneakers, monochrome tattoos on forearm
- A woman in a structured navy jumpsuit, tortoise glasses, holding an iPad against her chest

Their expressions: wide eyes, mouths slightly open, hands hovering mid-gesture. Pure deer-in-headlights. Nobody is touching the chainsaw. Nobody knows who put it there.

In the background, a 27" iMac screen quietly displaying a pristine Figma mockup of a UI, completely ignored.

Cinematic lighting: cool overhead studio daylight clashing with the warm orange glow seeping out of the chainsaw engine. Photorealistic, 35mm lens, shallow depth of field on the chainsaw, designers slightly soft. Slight atmospheric haze from the exhaust. Color palette: clean whites, warm woods, pop of safety-orange from the chainsaw.

Mood: comedic absurdity. The chainsaw is an alien object that doesn't belong here. The designers' world has just been crashed.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape for LinkedIn hero, or 4:5 portrait for LinkedIn feed.
