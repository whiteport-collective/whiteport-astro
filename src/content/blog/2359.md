Longing to talk to other designers about the how to stay relevant as a CX/UX/UI designer in the world of AI? **Tired of a world that only is about shallow demo-like products that look great and deliver nothing of true value for real users?**

Same here.

On **Monday December 22, 17:00–18:00 (CEST)** I’m hosting **WDS Sessions 1**: a live online jam where I’ll show how I use **AI agents + Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)** to design *with* code, not just pretty slides.

🎯 Made for designers who:
✨ Feel a bit “behind” in the AI madness
✨ Want to stay in control of strategy, UX and product decisions
✨ Want to go from *sketch → code* without spending their life prompting

In 60 minutes you’ll see how to:
🚀 Update your design process with an AI-agents framework that amplicy your design brilliance. 
🧠 Use agents as co-thinkers and create a conceptual specification that replace development prompting 
🎯 Make user research and usability testing and iterate your designs faster 
📐 Make strong design deliveries for development with AI or hand over to a Dev team.

**AI is not here to replace designers. WDS is here to replace pointless development prompting.**
Come jam with me and let’s design the future like adults, not just vibe around in Figma.

👉 **Sign up via the event page**: whiteport.com/blog/wds-jam-1/
