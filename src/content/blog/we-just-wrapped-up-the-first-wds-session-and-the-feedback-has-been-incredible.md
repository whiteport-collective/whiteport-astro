"insightful" doesn't even begin to cover it. In this session I expected another AI UI generator. What I saw instead was a complete paradigm shift in how we build products. 

The three features that genuinely surprised me: 

✨ Forget basic exports. WDS bridges the gap by delivering logic-ready specifications straight to the IDE. It turns "design intent" into "developer reality" instantly. 

✨ These aren't generic AI bots. They are specialised agents trained on the BMAD Method to ensure your design isn't just pretty, it's structurally sound and business-ready. 

✨ The moment I saw the GitHub repo, I realised this wasn't just a presentation—it's a production-ready workflow. It’s ready to be implemented today. 

If you’re a designer, product owner, or dev lead who feels like the gap between "the drawing" and "the product" is still too wide, you need to see this. It’s a glimpse into a workflow where the friction simply disappears.

If you’re a designer, product owner, or dev lead who wants to see a **frictionless workflow from concept to code**, this is it.

We’re just getting started, and 2026 is shaping up to be the year designers reclaim the strategic role in product development.
