Do you have a unique business? We are sorry!

No company is really the same. Even two companies indistinguishable for the outside observer do not operate in the same way. The vision by the founders, the leadership structure or just corporate traditions leads all organisations to function very differently. And this is a good thing.

Your corporate culture is not built overnight. Yours is probably built over time to support exactly your specific company in everything from how you acquire the best talent on the market to serve your specific target group. Your way of doing business is what makes you stand out in your market.

Can you now imagine how tone deaf it would be to steamroll some new and rigid processes onto your business without regard for the unique way you are doing things just because your new system demanded it? Would this not threaten to push you out of the delicate balance you manage to create with your uniqueness.

Therefore, Whiteports philosophy is that a system needs to be gently introduced over time in harmony with the people and processes involved. We in Whiteport simply do not see any positive outcomes in the cases where the business is forced to the system and not the other way around.

We feel truly sorry for all the countless people pulling their hair in frustration over new systems that simply don’t work in their business.
