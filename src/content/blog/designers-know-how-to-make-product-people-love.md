This is what changes everything.

**In WDS Session 1, we explored what happens when you’re forced to explain your ideas.
Component by component, decision by decision.**

When your product is generated from a clear specification, change becomes simple.
You don’t hunt through 500 lines of code.
You adjust the intent.
You reshuffle decisions.
You regenerate.

No spaghetti code.
No fragile fixes.
Just a system that stays coherent because it’s rebuilt from clarity every time.

This is what happens when code becomes a projection of strategy - not the other way around.
