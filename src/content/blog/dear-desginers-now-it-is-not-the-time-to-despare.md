I’m Mårten Angner — UX/CX/UI designer and founder of Whiteport and I want to show you how the BMAD-powered Whiteport Design Studio (WDS) that actually makes AI useful for designers. This isn’t about replacement — it’s about smarter, faster work that keeps you in control. ⚡️

🎯 **Join me on Monday 22nd December at 17:00–18:00 (CEST) for the first WDS Sessions 1 and find your designer edge! **

What we’ll cover:

- 🤝 A practical WDS workflow for strategy, project docs and alignment — with AI agents doing the heavy lifting.

- ✏️💻 Sketch-to-code: turn wireframes and designs into real, developer-ready specs.

- ♻️ Scalable design systems: build reusable components that play nicely with dev workflows.

- 🧩Better collaboration: use AI as a creative partner to speed ideas without losing craft.

📅 When: Monday, 22 Dec • 17:00–18:00 CEST 
🌐 Where: Online
🎤 Host: Mårten Angner

**Who should join: **designers, UX/CX/UI folks, design leads, product people and founders who want to level up. It’s free — grab your spot and let’s design the future together. ✨
