Did you attend one of my previous sketching courses? *Share your story and you can win a new sketching kit! ✨*. And the chance to win free admission to my new updated version of sketching course - Lines of thinking, Conceptual sketches meets AI. ✍️

**Back in 2007, I sketched my first concept with just pen and paper.**
The results surprised even me - immediate clarity, faster alignment and suddenly, clients and colleagues were saying "yes" to ideas that used to get lost in translation.

Before I knew it, people were asking me to teach them this straightforward approach. What started as a simple method became a movement,&nbsp;**transforming how 2,500+ designers, developers, and leaders communicate their ideas.**

I recently levelled up the technique with my experiences throughout the years sketching on tablett devices and adapted the technique to become more powerful communication technique for AI.
