There's one question that separates AI-generated decoration from actual design.

Most people never ask it.

Here it is: **"Why?"**

Why is the navigation on the left?
Why is the CTA blue?
Why is there a hero section at all?

An AI that can answer "why" is designing.
An AI that can't is decorating.

The difference isn't the output. It's the reasoning behind it.

Next time your AI generates a layout, ask it why. If it can't answer — you don't have a design agent. You have a screenshot generator.
