## As a desginer, I used to feel behind in the AI Madness

Hi, I’m **Mårten Angner**, founder of Whiteport and a UX designer with over 25 years of experience. Like many designers, I once felt lost in the AI revolution — unsure where we fit in and quietly wondering if our profession was being replaced. Every day, social media shouted the same message: *“AI is coming for your job.”*

Then,I discovered the **BMAD Method!**

I stumbled on this a free, open-source agent framework that works like a full development team, with you in charge. My expertise was amplified by the latest AI models, which didn’t replace me — they *10X-ed me*.

I loved it so much that I developed my own expansion pack — [**Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)** ](https://github.com/whiteport-collective/whiteport-design-studio)— a framework based on my own process for strategy and digital design process, refined over years of design experience, now supercharged by AI. It’s rocket fuel for designers who want to work smarter, faster, and with more creative control.

And now, I’m inviting you to come along.

I no longer feel like I’m catching up. I feel like I am leading from the edge of what’s possible. The best part? It’s free — shared with all my fellow designers out there. The world needs designers now more than ever.

## Design the Future — Beyond vibe coding.

We hear from designers every day who are frustrated about the new vibe coding tools that has taken the market with storm, not because it is new tools or that the new technology does not have its place. But basen on the insights in that great products are not created in 24 hours with a couple of prompts. 

We designers have often many years of experience in understanding businesses and their needs, Meet users in research and usability studies to be able to outline a seemless meeting between them in the interactive products. 

Instead of beeing invited, designer are just pushed aside and marginalized in the hunt for immediate satisfaction of something that looks shiny and great on the surface, but fail to deliver in reality. 

The result of not having designers on board, is product full of flair but that no one understands. 

## Find WDS! Invalueble design process in the form of AI Agents

Join us, and **Mårten Angner**, for an inspiring 60-minute session exploring the **Whiteport Sketch to Code Framework (WPS2C)**, a hands-on expansion for designers ready to move their craft into the development space and bring ideas to life directly in the IDE.

Built on the shoulders of giants, the proven **BMAD Method**, this approach empowers designers to take control of their workflow and collaborate seamlessly with both developers and AI.

## What You’ll Learn

- 🧠 **Leverage WDS:** Use the AI Agent framework to structure your design process for strategy, Project documents, Alignment documents and project contracts.

- 🤝 **Collaborate with AI:** Partner with AI as a creative co-thinker to refine ideas and translate them into clear, actionable specifications.

- ✏️ **Sketch to Code:** Turn your sketches, wireframes and designs into functional design specifications that becomes the product, since it replaces hours upon hours of pointless prompting in the implementation.

- 🧩 **Design Systems that Scale:** Build reusable, intelligent design components that integrate directly into developer workflows.

- 🚀 **Elevate Your Role:** Step into a new era of design — where creativity meets technology, and your ideas become instantly actionable.
