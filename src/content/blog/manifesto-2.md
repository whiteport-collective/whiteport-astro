From where we are standing, very seldom, a standard industry business system ends up being a sound investment.

Think about it, a business platform with the purpose of helping as many companies at the same time, needs to be vanilla flavoured as they are incentivized to have barely working features in as many areas as possible.

Catering to a specific client or even a specific type of company will alienate all other potential licence holders. Perfecting the system will also limit the need for costly extentina packages and specialised services oftentimes offered by the vendor.

Having a perfect system is just bad business, so they sell you on a bunch of sweet promises that you can not fully understand within a normal procurement process.

For us in Whiteport, it is self-evident, a standard system, designed for everyone, is really designed for no one
