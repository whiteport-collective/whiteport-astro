**Awaken your creative Artist at the Lines of Thinking Seminar**
📅 **4 June 2025** | 🕚 **11:00-14:00 **
📍 **The Park Forskaren (Stockholm) or Online**
🌍 **Language English**

**Discover how 3 hours can transform how you think, communicate and innovate.** 

No artistic skills required - just bring a pen, paper and your curiosity. In this hands-on session, you'll learn to:
✔ **Sketch ideas with clarity and speed** to solve problems and new learnings
✔ **Develop concepts visually** through storyboarding and rapid prototyping
✔ **Communicate complex ideas simply** to teams, clients and your favourite AI
✔ **Apply proven techniques** to think visually in any professional setting

**Why join?**
💡 **Created by Mårten Angner**, UX expert and this method has developed over time from its original analog version - to include pen enabled devices and now includes how to extend sketches to interact with AI.
🔥 **Trusted worldwide** by designers, managers, and technical leaders
🌐 **Flexible attendance** join us in Stockholm or participate online

**Limited seats available - reserve your spot today:** https://lines-of-thinking.confetti.events/
