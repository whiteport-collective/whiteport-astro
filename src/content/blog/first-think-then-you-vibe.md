AI makes it ridiculously fast to build things.  But, what if, now with AI, you just built the 𝑤𝑟𝑜𝑛𝑔 thing… faster? 🤔
Vibe-coding tools hand you that instant dopamine: “It runs!” ✅

But here’s the uncomfortable question:

What if you just built the *wrong* thing… faster?

What if your idea would have been **twice as good** if you’d spent one hour talking to real humans first? Domain experts. Users. The people who will actually live with the outcome.

Most workshop methods under the sun have the same hidden agenda:
**Stay in the problem long enough to understand it.**

A carpenter measures twice and cuts once.
A doctor diagnoses before prescribing.
But in the world of “vibe”… we often build before we think. And strategy is becoming a bit of a lost art in 2026.

If it’s a hobby project, fine. Ship, learn, laugh, repeat. 🎈
But if it’s a business-critical product inside a company, a headless sprint without user insight can get… expensive. Not just in money, but in trust, adoption, and momentum.

And honestly: **talking to real people is underrated fun.**
Most people *like* being asked for their expertise. It’s validating, energising, and it often turns into a surprisingly good conversation. Plus, you walk away with something no tool can generate for you: **context**. Nuance. Sharp edges. The “oh wow, I didn’t think of that” moments.

Built in to the **Whiteport Design Studio, you find a Trigger Mapping** module with Workshops hosted by our Analyst agent Saga and templates for using the result of the workshop to mold the product! This is an exercise we reccomend you before you build, regardless what tool you would like to use to build your product!

That’s why I’m hosting **WDS Session 2: Strategy in practise with WDS**.
We’ll talk about **Digital Strategy** and simple, practical ways to ask the right questions and capture the answers, so you can fit your strategy into a **complete one-slide foundation** before you design and build.
