I watched AI design a full dashboard in Figma.

It took 4 minutes. It looked professional.

Then I asked one question:
"Why is the navigation on the left?"

Silence.

We're building AI that can draw anything but can't explain a single decision.

That's not a design agent.
That's a fast intern with Figma access.
