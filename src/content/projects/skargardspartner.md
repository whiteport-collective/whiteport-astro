  - type: content-image
    heading: "About the project"
    text: |
      The main challenge we addressed was the lack of a simple and user-friendly time and job management tool for mobile use. Existing systems on the market were either too complex, costly, or disconnected from the company's own digital platform.

      Our solution was a service management system built using WordPress, WooCommerce, and the Whiteport Contract Plugin — wrapped in React-based apps for iOS and Android. The system runs directly on the client's own website and can be easily reused by other companies with similar needs.

      Main features include:
    list:
      - "A public ordering form connected to the internal booking system"
      - "Custom calendar views for planning, time reporting, and invoicing"
      - "Full job documentation handled on-site by the electrician"
      - "Instant mobile payments through Swish"
      - "Improved communication between electrician and client"

The app is built on WordPress with Whiteport's Contract Plugin and WooCommerce as a base, and is deployed in wrapper apps for both iOS and Android.
