    text: |
      After two years of technical development by the team at Greendog Innovation, the Hi Streamers platform was ready to prepare for launch. The solution is built for education, communication, and broadcasting, revolutionizing the video industry by making professional streaming easy, fast, and accessible to everyone.

      Whiteport was brought in to support the final stages of this journey — transforming the product from a strong backend engine into a polished, market-ready solution.

      Through close collaboration with the team, we quickly identified two key needs: a stronger, more accessible brand, and a clear way to present the videos created by their AI-driven video capture and editing platform.

      The result was **Hi Streamers** — a friendly, modern brand that balances professional tech with creative ease-of-use. We developed a full concept around the platform, including a streamlined **equipment booking system** and a **web-based video viewer** to showcase content created with the system.
