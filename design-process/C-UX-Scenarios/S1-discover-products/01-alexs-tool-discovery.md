# 01: Alex's Tool Discovery

**Project:** whiteport-astro
**Created:** 2026-03-12
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Discover that Whiteport builds open source design and agent tools, understand which product fits, and get to the install command.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Thought Leadership — Alex adopting WDS or WAS validates Whiteport as the authority on AI+design methodology. Every install is proof the thinking works.
**Objective:** Productized Offering — Product adoption directly. Alex using the tools creates the community that makes the offering sustainable.

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Alex (Primary)
**Situation:** Alex, 31, senior UX designer. Scrolling LinkedIn during lunch, sees a post from Mårten demoing an AI agent workflow that actually looks legit. Clicks the link. She's curious, not searching — this came to her.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** "This looks real — let me check it out and see if I can try it right now."

**Worry:** "Please don't be one of those 'book a demo' dead ends — just let me install the thing."

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Desktop
**Entry:** Clicks a product link from a LinkedIn post, lands on whiteport.com. She's in impulse-explore mode — she wants to see the product, not read about the company.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
Alex understands both WDS and WAS in under 2 minutes, identifies WDS as her starting point, and runs `npx whiteport-design-studio install` in her terminal within 5 minutes of landing.

**Business Success:**
One npm install, one GitHub star, one designer who will try WDS on her next project — and if it works, she'll tell her 400 LinkedIn connections.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **Start Page** — Lands here from the LinkedIn link. Sees the product prominently. Clicks "Products" or the product CTA.
2. **Products Hub** — Sees WDS and WAS. Quickly identifies which one matches what she saw in the post. Clicks into it.
3. **WDS Product Page** — Recognizes what she saw on LinkedIn. Finds the install command. Copies it. ✓

---

## Trigger Map Connections

**Persona:** Alex the AI-Curious Designer (Primary)

**Driving Forces Addressed:**
- ✅ **Want:** Have a system they can explain and teach to others — WDS is that system
- ✅ **Want:** Feel like a pioneer — installing open source tools before everyone else
- ❌ **Fear:** Fragmented information — the products hub shows a coherent ecosystem, not random tools
- ❌ **Fear:** Adopting the wrong approach — the tools are free and open source, low risk to try

**Business Goal:** Thought Leadership + Productized Offering
