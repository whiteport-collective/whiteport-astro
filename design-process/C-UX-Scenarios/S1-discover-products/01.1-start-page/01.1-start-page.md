## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Alex lands on whiteport.com and sees that Whiteport builds real products — not just an agency. The products need to be visible alongside the agency identity so Alex can find her way to them.

**Entry Context:** Clicked a link from a LinkedIn post by Mårten about an AI agent workflow. Lands on the homepage. She's in impulse-explore mode.

**User Situation (Q3):** Alex, 31, senior UX designer. Scrolling LinkedIn during lunch, saw a post demoing an AI agent workflow that actually looks legit. Clicked through. She's curious, not searching — this came to her.

**Mental State (Q4):**
- **Hope:** "This looks real — let me check it out and see if I can try it right now."
- **Worry:** "Please don't be one of those 'book a demo' dead ends — just let me install the thing."

**Discovery Method (Q6):** LinkedIn post link → whiteport.com homepage

**Exit Action:** Click "Products" in the nav, or click a product CTA in the page content.

**On-Page Interactions:**
- Scroll to see the products section on the homepage
- Scan nav items looking for the product she saw mentioned
