# Business Goals: Whiteport

> What does "winning" look like for Whiteport in the next 12 months?

**Created:** 2026-03-04
**Author:** Mårten Angner / Saga

## Goal 1: Become the Go-To Partner for AI-Powered Design

> _Whiteport is recognized as the expert you call when you want AI to actually work for your business — not just a buzzword, but a real workflow._

### SMART Objectives

1. **Book 2+ client discovery meetings per month** through whiteport.com within 6 months of launch
2. **Achieve Page 1 ranking** for "AI design methodology" and "AI-powered web design Sweden" within 12 months
3. **Convert 30%+ of discovery meetings** into signed projects

---

## Goal 2: Build a Thought Leadership Platform

> _Mårten and Whiteport are the names people share when someone asks "who actually knows how to combine design methodology with AI agents?"_

### SMART Objectives

1. **Publish 2+ stream posts per week** showcasing methodology, process, and results
2. **Grow newsletter/following** to 500+ engaged professionals within 12 months
3. **Get invited to speak/write** at 3+ industry events or publications

---

## Goal 3: Productize the Astro + AI Pattern

> _The website itself is the product demo. Clients see whiteport.com, understand the model, and want it for their own business._

### SMART Objectives

1. **Deliver 3+ client sites** using the Astro + AI agent pattern within 12 months
2. **Reduce per-project delivery time by 40%** through reusable patterns and agent workflows
3. **Achieve 90%+ client satisfaction** on the content management experience (ChatGPT + GitHub)

## Goal Hierarchy

```
Goal 1: Client Acquisition (Primary Outcome)
  ├── Goal 2: Thought Leadership (Prerequisite — builds trust and visibility)
```

Client acquisition is the ultimate measure of success. Thought leadership feeds the pipeline. The productized offering makes it sustainable.
