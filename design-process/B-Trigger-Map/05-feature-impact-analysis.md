## Decisions

**Must Have MVP (Primary High OR Top Tier Score):**
- Blog/Stream with real process content (13) — cornerstone of thought leadership
- The site itself as methodology showcase (13) — "the demo IS the product"
- About page — Mårten as a person (11) — trust signal for all groups
- Portfolio/case studies (11) — proof for Niklas, credibility for Alex
- Stream post gallery (11) — core Social Stream functionality
- SEO + Open Graph meta (11) — visibility for all goals
- WDS methodology overview page (8) — primary Alex draw
- Tag/category filtering (8) — Alex's exploration pattern
- Clear booking CTA (6, overridden) — direct revenue driver
- Services page (6, overridden) — Niklas conversion path

**Should Have:**
- Swedish language support (9) — capability flex, local market
- Client-side search (9) — improves exploration for Alex
- Social media post display (8) — Social Stream completeness
- Locked/preview content (6) — Lisa's workflow enhancement
- RSS feed (5) — Alex subscribes to content

**Defer:**
- Stream slider/carousel (4) — nice visual but not strategic
- Dark mode (3) — cosmetic, low impact
- Calendar view (1) — dropped from WP, not essential
- Analytics (1) — add when there's traffic to analyze

---

## Key Insight

The highest-scoring features all share one trait: **they prove the methodology works by existing**. The blog content, the site architecture, the portfolio, the gallery — they're not features to build, they're evidence to show. The site's primary job is to be an authentic demonstration of what Whiteport does.
