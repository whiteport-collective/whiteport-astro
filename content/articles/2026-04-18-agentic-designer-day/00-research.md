# Research: A Day in the Life of an Agentic Designer

## Syfte
Show how the designer's daily work has fundamentally shifted with AI agents. Not a "tools I use" post — a genuine reflection on how the rhythm, the skills, and the identity of the role changed. Relatable for designers and developers experiencing the same shift.

## Målgrupp
UX/UI designers, product designers, design leads, developers curious about AI workflows. People who feel the shift but haven't articulated it yet.

## Pillar
Behind the scenes

## Storyline
1. Hook: Forest walk photo — the new office
2. The old reality: email, calendar, meetings, sketches, feedback rounds, reiteration
3. The flip: what changed overnight
4. The new reality: briefing agents, directing, reviewing
5. The unexpected consequence: when I stop, everything stops
6. The new rhythm: 5-hour quota in 3 hours, then walk
7. The growth: broader scope, strategic thinking, writing code
8. CTA: How did your day shift?

## Nyckelpoänger
- Pre-AI: short bursts of creativity surrounded by ambassadorship
- Post-AI: the meetings just stopped
- New bottleneck: you, not the process
- New superpower: design → specify → develop → review → iterate, all in one person
