I cannot find a single job listing for a designer that does not list AI as a required skill.

This is not an isolated story. Design leaders tell me the same thing everywhere:

𝘐 𝘤𝘢𝘯𝘯𝘰𝘵 𝘨𝘦𝘵 𝘮𝘺 𝘥𝘦𝘴𝘪𝘨𝘯𝘦𝘳𝘴 𝘵𝘰 𝘦𝘮𝘣𝘳𝘢𝘤𝘦 𝘈𝘐. 𝘐 𝘨𝘢𝘷𝘦 𝘵𝘩𝘦𝘮 𝘢 𝘓𝘰𝘷𝘢𝘣𝘭𝘦 𝘱𝘳𝘰𝘵𝘰𝘵𝘺𝘱𝘦, 𝘮𝘺 𝘵𝘦𝘢𝘮 𝘳𝘦-𝘥𝘳𝘦𝘸 𝘵𝘩𝘦 𝘸𝘩𝘰𝘭𝘦 𝘵𝘩𝘪𝘯𝘨 𝘣𝘺 𝘩𝘢𝘯𝘥 𝘪𝘯 𝘍𝘪𝘨𝘮𝘢.

Developers, PMs, founders, design leaders. They all lean in. The resistance is designer-specific.

I think it runs deeper than job fear. 𝗜 𝘁𝗵𝗶𝗻𝗸 𝗶𝘁'𝘀 𝗮𝗯𝗼𝘂𝘁 𝗶𝗱𝗲𝗻𝘁𝗶𝘁𝘆.

I am not giving up on designers. I am asking designers to not give up on themselves.
