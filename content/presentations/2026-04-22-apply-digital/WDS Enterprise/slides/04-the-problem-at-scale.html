THE PROBLEM AT SCALE

The more AI you adopt,
the more coordination overhead you generate.

TRAP 01

The Figma Trap

Design sees AI output, takes it to Figma, spends weeks on pixel perfection. The code is already there. The habit wins.

TRAP 02

The Miro Trap

Strategy thinks in boards and documents. Smart people, great thinking — in formats no agent can act on.

TRAP 03

The Meeting Trap

Every handoff between strategy, UX, and development is a meeting, a summary, a Confluence page nobody reads.

Three teams, three tools, no connection. Add more AI tools, add more coordination overhead — unless you install a layer that handles it.
